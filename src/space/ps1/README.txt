oNotes:  Column by column
        Name is the exact game name, don't blame me for the spellings.
        HP is number of hit points. ATP is attack points (max=255)
        DFP is defence points (max 255). Exp is experience gained per monster
        Mes is number of Meseta gained per monster.
        Num is the maximum number you can meet in any one battle.
        Run is the chance of making a successful escape, high number is better
        max is 255.
        Trp is the chance of the treasure chest being trapped, low number is 
        less likely, max is 255.
        Win is ... what you will win.
        T is whether you can talk to them without magic.
        C is whether you can talk with magic (CHAT/TELE).
        R is whether you can ROPE them.
        P is whether you can PROTECT against them.
        M is a mystery column.
        Special is the special attack they can use.
